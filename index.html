Biosignal_and_image_processing

I created and analyzed various ECG (Electrocardiogram) signals using both STFT (Short-Time Fourier Transform) and FFT (Fast Fourier Transform). In the FFT analysis, I was able to analyze and record the electrical activity of the heart over time and transfer it into the frequency domain. The resulting representation shows the amplitude of these frequency components in both the time and frequency domains.

Computer_vision
